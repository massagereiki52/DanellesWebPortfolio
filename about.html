About Me

As an artist, I find inspiration in the day to day, but my biggest inspiration for my work is my faith. I try to incorporate at least a little bit of it into everything that I do. My designs aren't only inventive, complex, and inspirational, but they are also deep and have great meaning. I love the complexity of coding an elegant website and the process of photography. I have been writing for over 22 years. I have a blog called, "My Walk of Bold Faith," I not only have the experience in blogging, but I have also written content for various websites. It is my promise that through my skills and eye for detail, I will produce a product that you will be proud to show.
